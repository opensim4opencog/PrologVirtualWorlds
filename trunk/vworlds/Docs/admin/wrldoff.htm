Taking a World Offline

Taking a world offline allows you to run the world as an administrator. No other users can access the world when it is offline. You can take your world offline when you intend to make extensive changes to it. Another reason to take your world offline is to limit the number of hours per day or days per week that the world operates.

To take a world offline:

  1. In the Virtual Worlds Server application, click the title bar of the world you wish to take offline.
  2. Click the Tools menu, and then click Take World Offline.

After you perform this operation, all avatars in your world are logged off automatically and you will be able to make changes to the world. You should also note that after you have clicked this menu option, it changes to Put World Online.

To put the world back online:

  1. In the Virtual Worlds Server application, click the title bar of the world you wish to bring back online.
  2. Click the Tools menu, and then click Put World Online.
