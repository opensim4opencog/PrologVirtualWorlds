Adjusting Microsoft Virtual Worlds for Performance

You can make Virtual Worlds run faster and more smoothly by adjusting certain settings in the Options dialog box. You can choose to optimize your Virtual Worlds client for the best graphics quality (which may be slower, but produces richer graphics), or for best performance (which is faster, but produces graphics at a lower resolution.

To automatically download new avatar graphics:

  1. In the Virtual Worlds client, click Options on the menu bar. The Options dialog box appears.
  2. Click Auto download new avatar graphics.

To turn on your hardware acceleration:

  1. In the Virtual Worlds client, click Options on the menu bar. The Options dialog box appears.
  2. Click Turn off hardware acceleration.

    The other options for controlling performance disappear if you enable your hardware acceleration card. Hardware acceleration cards are disabled by default.

To turn off your hardware acceleration:

  1. In the Virtual Worlds client, click Options on the menu bar. The Options dialog box appears.
  2. Click Turn off hardware acceleration.
  3. If you want to speed up the Virtual Worlds graphic view of the world, then click Switch to lower resolution when moving.
