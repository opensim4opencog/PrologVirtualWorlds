Adjusting the Sound in Microsoft Virtual Worlds

Some virtual world environments may have sound effects as a background sound; sometimes sounds are attached to objects or events in the world. There is no volume control in Virtual Worlds, but you can use your computer's volume control to adjust the sound level.

You can toggle the sound off or on in the Options dialog box in the Virtual Worlds client. Sounds are on by default.

To turn off the sound:

  1. In the Virtual Worlds client, click Options on the menu bar. The Options dialog box appears.
  2. Click Play Sound Effects.
