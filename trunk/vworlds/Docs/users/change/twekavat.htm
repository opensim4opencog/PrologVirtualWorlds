Changing Your Avatar's Profile

Each avatar in a virtual world is represented by a graphical image and a profile. The profile is seen when you click on that avatar for more details. Your avatar has a profile as well, and you can use that profile to change any of the following information:

  • Appearance
  • Description
  • Details
  • Friends
  • Ignored

To change your avatar's profile:

  1. In the Avatars list, click your avatar, and then click the avatar profile menu item. (If your logon name is Jerry, this menu item will appear as Jerry's Profile.)
  2. In each section, change the information you want. If you make a mistake and want to start over, click Cancel. Any changes you have made will then be discarded.
  3. When you are satisfied with your changes, click OK.

To change your avatar's appearance:

  1. In the Avatars list, right-click your avatar, and then click the avatar profile menu item. (If your logon name is Jerry, this menu item will appear as Jerry's Profile.)
  2. Click on Appearance. The Profile window will show you how your avatar looks to other users.
  3. Select a new appearance from the drop-down menu to the right of your avatar's image. Your avatar's image will be updated in the Profile window instantly.
  4. When you are satisfied with your changes, click OK.
