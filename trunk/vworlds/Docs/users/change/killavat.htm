Deleting Avatars

When you delete an avatar, you delete all the information in that avatar's profile as well as that avatar.

To delete an avatar:

  1. On the Microsoft Virtual Worlds Start page, in the Nickname list, click the avatar you want to delete.
  2. Click Delete.
  3. Click OK.

The avatar is no longer be displayed in the Nickname list.
