Whispering

If you want to say something privately to one or more avatars in the room, you can whisper your message to them. Only the avatars you select in the Whisper dialog box see your whispered message. The Chat History pane shows, only to you and your whisper targets, the message that you whispered.

When another avatar whispers to you, the message appears in the Chat Window, but only to you and the other people specifically marked for that whisper message. For example,

Avatar1 whispers to Admin, "Purple walls would look better."

To whisper to another avatar:

  1. In the room, right-click the avatar you want to whisper to. You can also left-click the avatar's name in the Avatars pane and select Whisper....

    The Whisper dialog box appears.

  2. Check that the avatar you want to whisper to is listed in the Whisper To box.
  3. Type your whisper message into the Whisper dialog box.
  4. Click Send.

To whisper to a group of avatars at once:

  1. In the room, right-click the avatar you want to whisper to. You can also left-click the avatar's name in the Avatars pane and select Whisper....

    The Whisper dialog box appears.

  2. In the Whisper dialog box, click Show avatars in the world. Normally, you would only be able to whisper to avatars in the same room with you, but this selection puts all the avatars connected to the world in the same list. Now you can select specific avatars from this list to include in the group whisper message, whether they are in your room or not.
  3. Click Add to place their names in the Whisper To box. Click Remove to take avatars off of the list for this whispered message.
  4. Type in your message for the group whisper.
  5. Click Send.

To whisper to an avatar that is in another room in the world:

  1. In the room, right-click an avatar to activate the Whisper dialog box. You can also left-click avatar's name in the People pane and select Whisper. If there are no other avatars in the room with you, click your own name.

    The Whisper dialog box appears.

  2. In the Whisper dialog box, click the Show avatars in all rooms in this world. Now you can select specific avatars to include in the whisper message to the group.
  3. Click Add to place the avatar's name in the Whisper To box. Click Remove to take avatars off of the list for this whispered message.
  4. Type in your message for the avatar.
  5. Click Send.
