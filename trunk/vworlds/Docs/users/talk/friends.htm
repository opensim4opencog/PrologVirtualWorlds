Adding People to the Friends List

In Microsoft Virtual Worlds, you will probably meet many interesting avatars. You can save your favorite avatars' names in a Friends List in your own Profile. Friends can see information in your Profile that regular people cannot, if you set that information to the Friends level. For example, if you didn't want your age or location to be seen by all on your avatar's Details page, you could set the age and location fields to be visible only to Friends. Everyone else would not see age or location.

When you make a friend, the receiving avatar is asked if they want to accept or not. You may see this message yourself if someone wants to make you their friend.

To add a friend to your Friends List:

  1. Right-click on the avatar that you want to add to the list, or left-click the name of the avatar in the Avatars pane.
  2. Click Add to Friends List.

    A message is sent to that avatar that says, "Allow (NAME) to make you a friend?"
    If the other avatar clicks Yes, they are added to your Friends List.

    You can reach that list through your Profile. Their name changes to blue in the Avatars pane.
