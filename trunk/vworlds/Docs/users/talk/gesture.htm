Gesturing

One of the ways to add life to a conversation is by using gestures while you talk to other avatars. You have a variety of gestures to choose from, represented by small buttons marked in the avatar pane. When you click a gesture button, your avatar performs a sequence of motions, returning to the idle stance when finished. The Chat History pane shows that you performed a gesture.

Gestures are best viewed from the front of the avatar that is gesturing. You can also change your camera angle to place the camera in front of you, to see your own gesture. The gesture is also acted out in the avatar preview, which is displayed above the gesture buttons.

Different avatar models do different types of motions for each gesture. You can preview these when you select your avatar in the Profile menu, in the Appearance page. You can click gesture buttons as you preview the avatar model to see what they will do.

To gesture with your avatar:

  1. Move the mouse over the gesture buttons to choose the gesture you want to do.
  2. Click the gesture button. You can type in an emotion or posture text in the chat box and click the gesture button of your choice. Your emotion or posture text appears next to your name as you perform the gesture.
