Ignoring and Silencing People

As you join in conversation with other avatars in Microsoft Virtual Worlds, you may discover that you do not want to read the "spoken" comments from certain avatars. You can filter their "spoken" messages from your Chat window by adding that avatar to your Ignore List.

When an avatar is added to your Ignore List, that avatar graphic appears to be a cat stuffed in a bottle (to you.) You no longer see their messages and the room hears a message that you are ignoring that particular avatar. Other avatars can still "hear" the messages from the avatar you are ignoring.

The list of avatars that you are ignoring is in your avatar Profile. You can take people off the list there. The list is saved with your avatar even when you move to another room or another world, or log off and back on.

To ignore or silence an avatar:

  1. Right-click on the avatar that you want to ignore, or left-click the name of the avatar in the People pane.
  2. Click Ignore.

    A message is sent to that avatar that says, "(NAME) is ignoring you."
    That avatar is added to your Ignore List in your avatar's Profile.

To stop ignoring an avatar:

  1. In the People pane, left-click on your own avatar's name.
  2. Select Profile.
  3. Select Ignore List.
  4. Select the avatar's name and click Remove.
