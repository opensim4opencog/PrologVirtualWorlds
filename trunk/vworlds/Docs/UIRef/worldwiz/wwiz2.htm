Create a World Wizard, Step 2 of 4

This page helps you create an administrative avatar for your new world. The administrative avatar will be the official owner of the new world, and will have both Author and Wizard capabilities.

Wizard Options

Name

Type the name you want to use for the administrative avatar into this text box.

Password

Type the password for your administrative avatar into this text box. A password is mandatory for administrative avatars.

Confirm Password

Re-type your new password into this text box. This must match the password you gave above exactly, or you will not be able to go on to Step 3.

Automatically logon when wizard has finished

If you want the Create a World Wizard to bring up your new world in a client window and log your administrative avatar into it immediately after you have finished creating it, click to fill in this check box.
