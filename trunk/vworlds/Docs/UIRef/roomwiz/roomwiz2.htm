Room Wizard, Step 2 of 2

You can create a new room with the Room Wizard and add it to your world. You might want to do this if your world receives a lot of visitors who need more space to explore, for example, or you might want to create a private test room where you can experiment with new objects and scripts before adding them to the public rooms of your world.

With this step of the Room Wizard, you can pick the type and style of room you want to create.

Options

Rooms

Select a room type from this list. Every type of room listed has a different purpose. You might choose the Conference type for an online business meeting, or the Gallery type as a place where visitors can see images of your company's products and purchase them online. 

Styles

Select a style of room from this list. While the room type, as selected under the Rooms option, determines the purpose of the room, the room style determines the feel of the virtual room. Do you want your room to be formal or casual, contemporary or old-fashioned? You can make such decisions by selecting a room style from this list.

View of...

This pane displays a preview of the room type and style you have chosen, to help you make decisions about how your room looks and how it will look to visitors.

Description of...

This pane displays a brief description of the room type and style you have chosen, to help you make decisions about whether the selected type and style fit your needs.
