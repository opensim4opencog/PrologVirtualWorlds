Friends (Avatar Profile) Dialog Box

Use this dialog box to view your Friends list or delete people from it.

Options

Your Friends

This text field displays all the avatars in the world who are currently on your Friends list. Click an avatar's name to select it.

Remove

Click this button to remove the selected avatar from your Friends list.
