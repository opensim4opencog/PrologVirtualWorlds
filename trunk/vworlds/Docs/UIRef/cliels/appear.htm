Appearance (Avatar Profile) Dialog Box

Use this dialog box to select the appearance of your avatar, and see your avatar perform a variety of gestures.

Options

Your Avatar Appearance

Click the name of an avatar type in the scrolling list on the right side of the dialog box. An image of that avatar type appears immediately to the left of the list, above the gesture icons.

Gesture Icons

Click the gesture icons listed below to see what the selected avatar type looks like when it performs that gesture. In each case, the text others see in the world when you perform the gesture is given in boldface. For example, if your avatar's name is Cranky and you click the waving hand icon below, all avatars in the room see the text "Cranky waves," and those facing the front of your avatar see it wave its hand.
