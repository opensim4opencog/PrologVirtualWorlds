Avatar Shortcut Menus

Avatar shortcut menus contain actions you can use on an avatar to change it or get information on it. If you right-click an avatar, one of two menus appears, depending on whether you click your own avatar or another avatar. When the menu appears, you can drag to select an option.

Avatar Shortcut Menu Options for Your Avatar

You must be using a camera angle that includes your avatar in the main view to be able to click your own avatar.

Avatar's Profile

Your avatar's profile appears when you select this option. When it does, you can change your appearance, check your Friends list, or  use any other options contained in the profile. This option appears with your avatar's nickname instead of the word "avatar"; for example, if your avatar's nickname is Joe, the option appears on the menu as Joe's Profile.

Whisper...

Selecting this option makes the Whisper window appear. When it does, you can select other avatars and whisper to them. The Whisper window stays on top of the main view until you close it.

Avatar Shortcut Menu Options for Other Avatars

This menu appears when you click another avatar. You can only click avatars that are in the same room as your avatar.

Avatar's Profile

The selected avatar's profile appears when you use this option. When it does, you can look at parts of that avatar's profile, to see more information about the user. This option appears with the selected avatar's nickname instead of the word "avatar"; for example, if that avatar's nickname is Joe, the option appears on the menu as Joe's Profile.

Whisper To

Selecting this option makes the Whisper window appear. When it does, the selected avatar will already appear in the "Whisper To" box, and you can select other avatars to add to the list. The Whisper window stays on top of the main view until you close it.

Ignore

Selecting this option makes your avatar ignore the selected avatar. Ignoring an avatar causes it to appear to be inside a bottle, and prevents that avatar from talking to you again. The ignored avatar's nickname also appears in your avatar's Ignored list (within the Profile). If you right-click the ignored avatar, an option to remove the Ignore status appears.

Add to Friends List

Selecting this option sends the selected avatar a request to join your Friends list; if the avatar accepts, it is added to your Friends list automatically. This gives that avatar certain abilities, like being able to see information in your file that is limited to those on your Friends list.

Approach ->>

Selecting this option makes your avatar approach the selected avatar. This will usually bring your avatar face-to-face with the selected avatar, although it may bring you to a slightly different position, depending on the other avatar's settings.
