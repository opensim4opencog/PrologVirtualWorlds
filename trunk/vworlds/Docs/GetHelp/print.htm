Printing Help Topics

Within HTML Help, you can print a single topic, a topic and all its linked topics, a topic with a list of its linked topics, or all of the topics in a specific folder.

To print a single topic

  • Right-click in the topic window of HTML Help, and then click Print.

To print a topic and its links

  1. Right-click in the topic window of HTML Help, and then click Print.
  2. Click Print all linked documents, and then click OK.

To print a topic and a list of its links

  1. Right-click in the topic window of HTML Help, and then click Print.
  2. Click Print table of links, and then click OK.

To print all the topics in a folder

  1. On the Contents tab of HTML Help, right-click the folder whose contents you want to print, and then click Print.
  2. Click Print everything contained in the current heading, and then click OK.
